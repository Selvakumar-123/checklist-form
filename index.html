Boomlift Daily Checklist

Take note !!

1. Only trained and certified operators should use a boom lift.

2. Wear hard hats, full body safety harnesses, and non-slip footwear.

3. Always hook your safety harness to designated anchor points.

4. Operate on stable and even surfaces.

5. Check for power lines, ceilings, beams, or tree branches before ascending/descending.

6. Don't leave the key on the boom lift; return it to the security guard after use .

7. Failure to return/loss of key, platform housekeeping will result in a $50 admin fee issued .
